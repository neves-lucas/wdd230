Attractions

Pajuçara Beach features clear waters and coral reefs, as well as many beachfront restaurants and bars. The vibrant Praia Ponta Verde is great for surfing and nightlife. Dunas de Marapé has impressive sand dunes perfect for photos or sandboarding along Gunga beach lagoon. The Maceió Seaquarium houses various marine life like turtles and sharks. From the Jaraguá Lighthouse you can take in beautiful city views. The Pajuçara Beach Market open-air market sells local art, crafts, and food. You can also take boat trips to nearby islands with pristine beaches prime for snorkeling and relaxation. At night, check out the Gothic-style Maceió Cathedral lit up downtown.
