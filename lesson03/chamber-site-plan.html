MCZ Chamber of Commerce

MCZ is an abreviation of the city named Maceió, in which the chamber is hosted.

Description

The MCZ Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.
